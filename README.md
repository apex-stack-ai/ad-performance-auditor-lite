# Ad Performance Auditor Lite — Free Claude Skill

Audit your ad campaigns on 3 key performance dimensions. Get instant, actionable recommendations to improve ROAS.

## What This Does

Paste your campaign data and get scored on:

1. **Creative Performance** — Are your ads fatiguing? CTR trends, frequency analysis, creative rotation recommendations
2. **Budget Allocation** — Is your spend distributed optimally? Efficiency scoring across campaigns and ad sets
3. **ROAS Analysis** — Are you hitting benchmarks? Performance vs. industry averages with scaling recommendations

## Quick Start

1. Download the `.skill` file from this repo
2. Install in Claude (drag and drop or use `/install`)
3. Paste your campaign data or describe your campaigns
4. Get an instant 3-dimension audit with scores and action items

## Example

```
Audit my Meta campaign: spent $5,000 last week across 3 ad sets, 
getting 2.1x ROAS. CTR dropped from 1.8% to 1.2% over the past 2 weeks.
```
